FAQ

What is the Roblox Skin Maker?

The Roblox Skin Maker allows you to create and customize your own skins, including t-shirts, for your Roblox avatar. Simply link your account and start designing!

How do I use the Roblox Skin Maker?

Enter your Roblox username and password to link your account, then a window will popup and start designing your own wonderful skins!

Is my account information safe?

Yes, your account information is securely handled and used only for linking your account to the skin maker for transferring the skins you make to your account.
